# 3. Navigating the Jupyter Interface

## Dashboard

- Lists your files and notebooks.
- Create a new notebook: Click "New" > "Python 3".

## Notebook Operations

- Rename: Click the notebook title at the top.
- Save: Click the floppy disk icon or press `Ctrl+S`.
- Checkpoints: Jupyter automatically saves checkpoints.

**Try:** Create a new notebook and rename it to `my_first_notebook.ipynb`.
