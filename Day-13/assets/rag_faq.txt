Q: What documents can I use?
A: Any local .txt files. You can also pre-process PDFs into text.

Q: How big should chunks be?
A: 500–1000 characters with 20–30% overlap works well.

Q: Which embedding model?
A: text-embedding-3-small (fast, low cost) is fine for this exercise.

Q: Which LLM model?
A: gpt-4o-mini via the Responses API.
